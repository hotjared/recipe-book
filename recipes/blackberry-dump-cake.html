Blackberry Dump Cake

Description

This blackberry dump cake is a crowd pleaser. Serve with ice cream or whipped topping.
Much like blackberry pie but instead its much neater and can feed a whole party!

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 cups of fresh blackberries
  • 1 1/2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter, thinly slices
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package French vanilla cake mix

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix blackberries and sugar together in a bowl. Transfer to a
    9x13-inch glass baking pan and spread evenly over the bottom.
  3. Sprinkle cake mix evenly over the berries. Place the butter slices
    evenly over the cake mix; most of the cake mix should be covered with butter slices
    do not mix or stir.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until topping is golden brown,
    about 45 minutes. Serve warm.
